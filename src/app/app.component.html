CLOSE-OLOGY MAP
To use the Close-ology map, simply enter the data requested in the fields below. The map is populated with color coded well points within a predetermined radius of the location selected. The color of each well point reflects an estimated ROI, which in turn is based on the parameters you entered.

Terms:
Oil and Gas price: Choose an oil and gas price and enter it here. Remember, this map is for illustrative purposes only and the oil and gas price you chose will not change for the life of the investment. Since oil and gas prices fluctuate daily the model will not reflect ultimate returns.

Production History By Months: This number breaks down the ROI of the well you selected over the first 12, 24, 60 and last 12 months of production. Remember ROI, and in turn Production History by Months, is only reflective of the parameters you select, i.e. gas price, oil price, production expenses, daily production etc.

Green Circle: The number in the green circle on the left of the page is your OWS Score. It reflects the combined estimated average ROI for all of the well points in the Close-ology map, based on your entered parameters, assuming the data points you entered hold true.
